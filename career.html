What sets us apart
Employee well being comes first

You can only be on the top of your game when you're feeling your best, and when you're not, we'll help you get there! Apart from access to wellness services (yoga sessions, counseling), we also offer generous mental health leaves for when you need them.

Complete ownership through ESOPs

At UC, not only do you have complete ownership in everything that you do, you also get to be a part owner with our robust ESOP program. Our business is just as much yours.

Opportunity to serve the community

Our mission is to empower millions of service professionals, the world over. Providing them a platform, enabling them as microentrepreneurs and ensuring the best earnings possible, it’s what keeps us going.

Culture that inspires and celebrates

We admit it, we’re picky. We have a very high bar on talent which we continuously raise, so get ready to be surrounded by the best minds, continuously inspired and always celebrating our very own UC Champs.

Unparalleled growth and development

We believe in learning by doing. Whether it is launching a new category, entering a new market, working across functions or pods. We offer limitless opportunities to our people to build their skills and fuel their growth.

More than a place to work

From vaccination drives for our partners, employees and their families to health insurance, staying connected through fun activities (meme wars and more!) and offsites to unwind. We go above and beyond for our Urban Tribe and we wouldn’t have it any other way.

Culture

Our values define who we are, what we want to achieve and most importantly how we’re going to do it. They create our collective path and guide our every step, every action. When in doubt, they’re the clarity we need and when we’re on our way to achieve big things, they’re the confirmation we want.

Urban Company’s journey

2014

3 friends, a few drinks (or cups of coffee?) and 1 solid idea later, Urban Company (then UrbanClap) was launched as a lead generation marketplace

Early 2015

Kaching! UC raised $10 million funding and went places-Delhi, Bangalore, Mumbai and Chennai

Late 2015

UC launched its full-stack model in beauty. This makeover resulted in massive growth, from 0 to $2 million ARR

2017

All in! The "full-stack model" model was expanded to other categories like Spa, Appliance Repair, Electricians, Plumbers and Carpenters

2018

UC set out on a new adventure with an international expansion in Dubai & Abu Dhabi

2019

Even the land down under- Australia

2020

With more to explore, UC launched in Singapore

2021

New achievement level unlocked: Unicorn status with a valuation over $2 billion
